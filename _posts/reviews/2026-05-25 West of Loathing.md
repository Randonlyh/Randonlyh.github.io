# West of Loathing

Some gags overstayed their welcome a bit and there's probably a few more areas than needed since some of them can kinda blend together and can be quite similar (i found like 66) but that was the exception rather than the rule, most gags landed and most areas had some differentiating factor. Gameplay isn't that complicated but it's enough to keep things interesting even if it is really quite easy. Music is fitting for the vibe of this game even if there is nothing that memorable here. Graphical style is charming and was a unique treat. Linux native port (rare) is also very cool thank you devs.

So yeah overall, the game is quite short and there's humour at every turn, you can't go wrong with it really.
