# Birds Organised Neatly

Despite the low rating (4/10) I do actually like this game, it's just not very mechanically deep compared to... pretty much everything else I've played.

My biggest complaint with the gameplay probably just comes down to a bit of QoL. I think a hint option that reveals the spot of a bird would've done wonders to help make the game nicer, because while it's kind of them to provide a skip level button, I never pressed it since skipping a level would've make me feel bad... But sometimes I was stuck on certain levels for quite sometime, in which case I would look up the solution _but_ would only look at 1 or 2 bird positions and tried to figure it out from there. It made the games pacing a lot nicer and didn't feel cheap to me.

Aside from that, uhhhh idk. It's just a puzzle piece fitting game. Very cosy and warm vibes graphically and audio wise, and all the birds were cute! Good time waster for a few hours.
