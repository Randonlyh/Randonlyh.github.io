# Flow Free

I beat (and perfected) every single level (that was free). I do not recommend doing this. I will review this game as if it was a single player game with a campaign or something, but keep in mind this is just a little mobile game.

This game is a nice time waster for the most part and it's a good puzzle game, but if you aim to finish it the novelty of this game wear offs just due to how long everything sticks around before getting switched up. This isn't initially a big deal if you just play this in short bursts (like I mostly did), but even while doing that you will eventually get into a habit of opening the game, realising you're still doing the same kind of puzzle and then closing it very shortly after.

This becomes the most egregious in the final few packs, where there aren't any gimmicks and it's just pure flowing. Please tell me, who decided to make 2 packs full of 450 levels each instead of 150 at the very end? And this is right after 3 packs of some of the largest levels in the game (so they take forever) and 3 packs of what are meant to be some of the most difficult levels (so they take forever). Genuinely the most demotivating and boring end to a game ever. At least I felt super duper happy once I completed the last level and it was finally over I suppose.

Part of me is tempted to buy the £5 DLC of another 1920 levels just cause it would be funny, but no, I've definitely more than had my fill. It's time to get a new game to play on my phone.
